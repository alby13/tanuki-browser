How To Download Tanuki Browser for AMD64

Please navigate to the main GitHub: https://github.com/alby13/tanuki-browser

On the right menu you will see a link for "Releases" - Click on "Releases"

Find the post Tanuki Browser Version [Number] - Linux (AMD64)

On the bottom you will see "Assets". Expand this if needed.

Download: tanukibrowser-v[number]-amd64.tar.gz
